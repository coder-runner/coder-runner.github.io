Aidan Nisbett - Writer

About Me

Aidan Nisbett is a Junior at Umass Amherst studying English focosuing on creative, technical, and journalistic writing. On top of his major, he is pursuing a certificate in Profssional Writing and Technical Communication paired with an IT minor, as well as a TESOL for teaching English to non-native speakers abroad. At the moment, he is working on his honors thesis which will comprise of a collection of short stories surrounding themes of revenge, fate, and existentialism.

My Story

Aidan started as Mechanical Engineer at UMass Lowell, until he took a writing course which catalyzed his switch to an English major. As part of the change, he transferred to UMass Amherst, where he balances his studies between literature, both historical and contemporary, and creative writing, typically fiction.

Currently, Aidan participates in writing workshops where he improves his narrative writing and editing capabilities with his fellow peers. He is also a writing intern for Catalyst Planet, a travel/global events newsletter which focuses on cultures, places, and people often overlooked or underdiscussed.

When Aidan isn't writing you can find him running with friends, hiking, or playing video games.
